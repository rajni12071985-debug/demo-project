💡 Security Awareness Tips

Never Share OTPs

Bank officials or authentic organizations will never ask you for your verification codes or OTP passwords.

Inspect Main Domains

Review the main URL domain carefully. Fraudsters rely on misspellings such as 'q00gle.com' or 'paypaI.com'.

Don't Open Unknown Links

Avoid clicking links placed in unsolicited emails. Be extra skeptical if the message tries to create a false sense of urgency.

Verify True Senders

Do not simply check the display name. Validate the actual underlying email address of the sender using client details.
